Superman

Man Of Steel

" The 'Amazing' can only be created by facing fear, risk, and failure during the process."-Clark Kent

Clark Kent (Kryptonian Name Kal-El), as known as Superman, is probably the most iconic superhero of all time.

He starred in "Man Of Steel" in 2013, which is the first ever DCEU film. He struggled between being the beacon of the world or staying normal as a farmboy.

His love interest, Lois Lane is an award-winning reporter who was saved by superman during her her report. He is portrayed by Henry Cavil.

He was defeated and dead in "BvS" but he MIGHT OR MIGHT NOT(spoiler free!) return in the new movie.
