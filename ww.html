Wonder Woman

Princess Of themyscira, Warrior From The Past

" I will fight for those who cannot fight for themselves."-Diana Prince

Diana Prince, as known as Wonder Woman, is a Amazonian Warrior Princess from Themysiscira. Her mother, Hippolyta, is the Queen of the Amazons, a tribe of women warriors made by Zeus.

She was introduced in "Batman VS Superman: Dawn of Justice" and starred in her solo movie "Wonder Woman", which is a huge success and a major blockbuster in the summer of 2017.

Her love interest, Steve Trevor sacrificed himself during the First World War, which made Diana devasated and made her who she is today. She is portrayed by Gal Gadot.
