Bartholomew Henry "Barry" Allen, as known as The Flash, is a regular guy who was struck by lightening and got the power of superhuman speeds.

He was introduced in "Batman VS Superman: Dawn of Justice" and "Suicide Squad" but only with minor roles. His major DCEU debut is going to be in the "Justice League". It is rumored that this character is going to get a solo movie in 2020.
