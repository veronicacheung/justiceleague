Aquaman

King Of Atlantis

" “IT’S ON HIM.”"-Arthur Curry

Arthur Curry is the reluctant ruler of the underwater kingdom of Atlantis. He is half-human and half-Atlantian (People in Atlantis are called Atlantians). He has this future wife of him called Mera that is very pretty. The solo movie of Aquaman's will be out at early 2018. He is portrayed by Jason Momoa, who is Hawaiian and very attached to the ocean.
