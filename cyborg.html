Cyborg

96% Robot, 4% Human

"Relax Alfred, I'll take it from here."-Victor Stone

Victor Stone, a formal all-star football player, is a "deceased" 96%-robot-4%-human superhero. He lost most of his body after a tragic accident so his father replaced most of his body into mechanical parts. His cybernetics technology helped the team to defeat their enemies.

He first appeared in "Batman Vs Superman: Dawn Of Justice" in the DCEU universe. He is portrayed by Ray Fisher.
